Avacado Tuna Salad

Description

Here's a recipe for a tasty, high-protein favorite.

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Avocado
  • Fruits and Vegetables
  • Walnuts
  • Pickle Juice
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Cut avocado in half, scoop out the flesh into a large bowl, and mash avocado with the back of a fork or potato masher.
  3. Add tuna, apple, onion, celery, toasted walnuts, mayonnaise, pickle juice, dill, Dijon mustard, cumin, salt, and pepper; mix well.
